Our Diagnostic Services

Clinical Pathology

Comprehensive analysis of bodily fluids including blood, urine, and other samples to diagnose diseases.

Hematology

Specialized testing for blood disorders, complete blood counts, and coagulation studies.

Microbiology

Identification of infectious organisms through cultures, sensitivity testing, and molecular diagnostics.

Biochemistry

Analysis of chemical components in body fluids to assess organ function and metabolic processes.

Histopathology

Microscopic examination of tissue samples to detect abnormalities, including cancer diagnosis.

Immunology

Testing for immune system disorders, allergies, and autoimmune conditions.

Molecular Diagnostics

Advanced genetic and molecular testing for personalized medicine approaches.

Radiology

State-of-the-art imaging services including X-ray, ultrasound, and CT scans.

Our Commitment
