Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and procedures

What are your operating hours?+

Our standard operating hours are:

  • Monday - Friday: 7:00 AM - 8:00 PM
  • Saturday: 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM
  • Sunday: 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM
  • Emergency services: 24/7 available
Do I need a doctor's referral for testing?+

While many tests require a physician's order, we also offer direct-to-consumer testing for certain screenings. Please contact us to determine if your desired test requires a referral.

What safety measures do you have in place?+

We follow strict safety protocols including:

  • Regular facility sanitization
  • Personal protective equipment for staff
  • Social distancing in waiting areas
  • Sterile equipment for all procedures
  • Infection control compliance
How do I schedule an appointment?+

You can schedule appointments through:

  • Online booking on our website
  • Phone: (555) 123-4567
  • In-person at our facility
  • Through your referring physician
What is your cancellation policy?+

We require 24-hour notice for appointment cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee. Emergency situations are considered on a case-by-case basis.

What should I bring to my appointment?+

Please bring:

  • Photo identification
  • Insurance card (if applicable)
  • Physician referral form (if required)
  • List of current medications
  • Previous test results (if available)
How long does it take to get test results?+

Turnaround times vary by test:

  • Routine tests: 24-48 hours
  • Specialized tests: 3-7 days
  • Genetic testing: 10-14 days
  • Critical results: Communicated immediately
How will I receive my test results?+

Results are available through:

  • Our secure patient portal
  • Your referring physician
  • Mail or secure email (upon request)
  • In-person pickup
Do I need to fast before my test?+

Fasting requirements depend on the specific test:

  • Lipid panel: 12-hour fast required
  • Basic metabolic panel: 8-12 hour fast recommended
  • Glucose test: Fasting required as per physician instructions
  • Most other tests: No fasting required

Specific instructions will be provided when you schedule your appointment.

What insurance plans do you accept?+

We accept most major insurance plans including Medicare and Medicaid. Please contact our billing department at (555) 123-4567 to verify your specific insurance coverage.

What payment methods do you accept?+

We accept:

  • Cash
  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • Personal checks
  • Health savings account (HSA) cards
  • Insurance payments
Do you offer payment plans?+

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for patients without insurance or with high out-of-pocket costs. Please speak with our billing department to discuss available options.
