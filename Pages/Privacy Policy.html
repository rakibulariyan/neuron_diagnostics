Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

1. Information We Collect

At Neuron Diagnostic, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, contact details, date of birth, gender, medical history
  • Health Information: Medical records, test results, treatment history
  • Payment Information: Billing details, insurance information
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal and health information to:

  • Provide diagnostic services and medical care
  • Process payments and insurance claims
  • Communicate test results and appointments
  • Improve our services and patient care
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Your referring physician or healthcare provider
  • Insurance companies for claim processing
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Accredited laboratories for specialized testing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures including:

  • Encrypted data storage and transmission
  • Secure access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Employee training on data protection

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal and health information
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your information (where permitted by law)
  • Object to certain processing activities
  • Receive your data in a portable format
