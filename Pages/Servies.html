Our Diagnostic Tests

Complete Blood Count (CBC)

Measures different components of blood including red cells, white cells, and platelets.

Sample: Blood Report: 4-6 hours

Basic Metabolic Panel

Evaluates glucose level, electrolyte and fluid balance, and kidney function.

Sample: Blood Report: 6-8 hours

Urinalysis

Examines the physical, chemical, and microscopic properties of urine.

Sample: Urine Report: 2-4 hours

Lipid Profile

Measures cholesterol and triglyceride levels to assess heart disease risk.

Sample: Blood Report: 24 hours

Liver Function Test

Assesses the health of your liver by measuring enzymes, proteins, and substances.

Sample: Blood Report: 24 hours

Thyroid Profile

Measures thyroid hormone levels to evaluate thyroid gland function.

Sample: Blood Report: 24 hours

Cancer Marker Panels

Comprehensive testing for various cancer biomarkers for early detection and monitoring.

Sample: Blood/Tissue Report: 3-5 days

Cardiac Risk Assessment

Autoimmune Disease Panel
